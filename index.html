01 / About

I live in Salem, Oregon and work as Software Consultant at Dynatrace. I develop, deploy, and optimize software solutions that give customers deeper visibility into their cloud environments and applications.

I enjoy building things that outlast a single project — reusable internal apps, automation that removes manual and repetitive work, and solutions that make the next problem easier or eliminate it entirely. I like working through challenging technical problems and spend time on my own continuing to learn.

When I'm not working, I'm usually hiking somewhere in the Pacific Northwest or tending to my plant collection. I love Oregon but am open to relocating elsewhere on the West Coast if the right opportunity comes up.
